PhotoSync for Facebook

A browser extension to sync data from your Facebook photos to an external destination of your choosing.

How it works

When you visit one of your own Facebook photos, the photo ID and comment data is synced to the service that you configure. Because it is a browser extension, the service can be local to your own network, or a cloud service you set up.

What it is not

It is not a bot or an automated scraper. It only copies the photo data on the page, only for your own photos, and saves it for later.

We do not collect your photo data, and do not even run analytics in the extension.
